Frequently Asked Questions

Browse our menu, select a drink or food item, customize your size, strength, flavor and add-ons. Tap "Add to Cart", then head to your cart to review and checkout.

Once an order has been placed, it cannot be modified. However, you can contact our support team immediately and we will do our best to accommodate your request before preparation begins.

Tap the heart icon on any menu item or on the product detail page. You can view all your favorites from the Favorites tab in the bottom navigation.

We offer four sizes: Small (8 oz), Medium (12 oz), Large (16 oz), and Extra Large (20 oz). Medium is selected by default. Each size up adds a small surcharge.

Go to your Order History from the Profile page, find the order you want and tap the "Reorder" button. All items from that order will be added to your cart.

Go to Profile and tap the edit icon next to your name. You can update your name, email and phone number from the Edit Profile sheet.

You can reach us via Live Chat (available 9 AM – 9 PM), email at support@pickbrew.com, or call us at (555) 123-4567.
